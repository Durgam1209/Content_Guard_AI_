ContentGuard AI 2
We all know the age ratings on movies: PG, PG-13, R. But have you ever stopped to think about how those decisions are actually made?

Today, classifying a movie is a slow, manual process. Humans (often a classification board like the MPAA in the US or the CBFC in India) have to sit and watch every single minute of a movie. They take careful notes on every swear word, every fistfight, and every intimate scene. Then, they argue about the context before finally deciding on an age rating.

This process is subjective, expensive, and takes a long time. This is exactly the problem ContentGuard AI 2 solves.

What is ContentGuard AI 2?
ContentGuard AI 2 is an automated movie rating and content certification platform.

Think of it as a highly trained, super-intelligent film critic. It can "watch" a movie or "read" a script, automatically flag every single concerning scene, and tell you exactly what age rating it should receive in different countries.

How it Works (The Three-Step Process)
The project follows a simple, logical flow that transforms a raw video into a finished report:

1. THE INGESTION
The user uploads their movie file or pastes in their script. The system immediately gets to work organizing the data for the AI.

2. THE "MULTI-MODAL" ANALYSIS
This is the "brain" of the project. The AI (powered by Gemini) does three things at once:

Visuals: It watches the video frames for visual triggers (like weapons or gore).

Audio: It listens to the audio for tone and intensity.

Text: It reads the text of what characters are saying to detect profanity.

Crucially: It understands context—it can tell the difference between a joke and an actual threat.

3. THE RESULTS & CERTIFICATION
Once the AI finishes "watching," the dashboard fills up with useful info:

The Timeline: A visual graph showing exactly when the movie gets intense.

The Triggers: A detailed list with precise timestamps (Example: 01:22:15 - Violence: Person punched).

The Final Report: The AI generates a legally compliant Certification Report. It tells you: "This movie is NC-17 in the US because it contains X minutes of graphic violence."

Why This Matters
By using advanced AI, ContentGuard AI 2 makes the movie industry smarter, safer, and faster. It helps:

Film Studios: They get their rating faster and know exactly what to cut to lower their rating (for example, moving from an 'A' rating down to a 'UA-13+' to reach more viewers).

Classification Boards: The AI does the "heavy lifting" of watching and logging, so human reviewers can focus only on the most difficult decisions.

The Global Market: A single click shows how a movie will be rated in the US, India, UK, and Germany, making it easy to sell movies internationally.
